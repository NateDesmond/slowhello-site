How it works

A month of small moments, one big gift.

1

Choose who and when

Pick someone you love and a future day. A birthday, an anniversary, a graduation.

2

Write your message

Write a short message and we split it into one word a day, paced to land right on the day.

3

Record a word a day

Say that day's word in a quick video. On the day, the clips stitch into their gift.

Features

Built to be sincere, and private.

🔒

Private by design

Everything stays on your device. No account, no servers, no data collected. Your words are just for them.

📅

A warm word calendar

A gentle calendar of the words you have said so far, so you can watch the message take shape.

🔔

A gentle daily reminder

A soft nudge each day to record that day's word. A few seconds is all it takes.

🎁

A dated delivery card

The finished gift carries the proof of the effort, so they can see how long you have been at it.
